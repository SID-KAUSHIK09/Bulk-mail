First you have to turn on 2 step verification

  1. Open your google account
  2. Click on "Security" from left side.
  3. After that, you can see 2-Step verification is off, hence hit on 2-Step verification.
  4. Complete all steps for 2step verification, finally it'll be turned on.

Now you have to create Google app password

  1. Firstly, open your Google Account and hit on Security option.
  2. Click on App Password, you can see app password is none for now.
  3. Thereafter, enter your Google account credentials and hit on Next.
  4. Select App accordingly from Mail, Calendar, Contacts, YouTube, and Other.Click on mail here.
  5. Now pick a device as per your need from the drop-down list i.e., ­­iPhone, iPad, BlackBerry, Mac, Windows Phone, Windows Computer, and Other.
  6. After that, click on Generate to start the operation.
  7. 16-digit passcode is generated successfully for your Google account, press Done to confirm.
  8. This 16-digit passcode is your App's Password. Save it somewhere for future use.
